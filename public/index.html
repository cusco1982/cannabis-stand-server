Introduction

You have 7, 14, or 30 days to make as much money as possible selling 8-Bit Cannabis! You’ll have complete control over pricing, inventory, marketing, security and supplies.

Secret Sauce to success: You will start with $5000 to begin purchasing your inventory, and overhead costs to start selling 8-Bit Cannabis. Make sure you buy enough of everything, or you won’t be able to sell if you run out!

Weather: This plays a big role in customer demand. Read the weather report every day! When the temperature is low, or the weather is bad (overcast, cloudy, rain), don’t expect to sell as much,and buy accordingly. Set your prices higher on hot, sunny days to increase profit, even if you sell less 8-Bit Cannabis.

Customer Satisfaction: As you sell your 8-Bit Cannabis, people will decide how much they like or dislike it. If your popularity is low, fewer people will buy, even if the weather is hot and sunny. But if you increase your popularity, you’ll do okay even when it’s cold and rainy!

At the end of 7, 14, or 30 days you’ll see how much money you made. Try to beat your high score!

Inventory/Purchasing Help

It’s hugely important to manage your inventory well, or you’ll quickly go out of business. How much you need depends on a few things.

Number of customers: If the weather is bad, and your price is high, don't expect more than 15-25 customers. But if the weather is hot and clear, and your prices are reasonable, expect 50-75.

Glass Jars and 8-Bit Cannabis: You need one glass jar per customer, plus however many grams of 8-Bit cannabis you decide to put in each jar.

Flyers and Security Guards: The number of flyers you give out in a day will affect how many customers come to your stand. More flyer$ = More customer$$ The number of security guards you have will affect how many customers you see. Customers want to feel safe when making their purchases, more $ecurity = More Customer$

It's always better to buy a little extra than to not have enough.

A few more tips: Your glass jars won’t go bad, so always have plenty. Your security guards are just available for the day and you will need to rehire the guards each day. Same goes for flyers, you need to purchase marketing advertisements in order to reach your customers on a daily basis. Your 8-Bit Cannabis will usually be OK for a few days, but may sometimes be smoked by employees!

Price/Quality Control Help

As the weather becomes hotter and clearer, demand for 8-Bit Cannabis goes up and customers will pay more for it. In bad weather, customers won't pay as much.

The number of grams you decide to put in each glass jar will affect the demand of your product and the happiness of your customers. Most customers will expect at least 3 grams per jar. You can reduce that to cut costs, but you’ll also reduce demand. Also, by adding more 8-Bit Cannabis to each jar, you’ll make a better product that customers will pay more for.

The amount of flyers you are able to give out each day will determine the amount of customers you see in a day. The security guards provide safety and a sense of assurance for your customers. You want them to feel safe so that they come back often to get more 8-Bit Cannabis!
